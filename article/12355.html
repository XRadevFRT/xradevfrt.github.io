Rescue Teams Reach Stranded Expedition

All 12 members safe after helicopter evacuation from remote region.

Published 22 Feb 2026 Â· 2 min read

All 12 members of the expedition have been evacuated to safety after rescue teams reached their camp. Helicopters ferried the group out following a multi-day operation hampered by severe weather.

Family members have been informed. The expedition leader thanked emergency services and said the team was in good spirits despite the ordeal.
