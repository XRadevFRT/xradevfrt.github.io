Parliament Passes Landmark Healthcare Reform

Bill expands coverage to millions; opposition vows to challenge in court.

Published 25 Feb 2026 Â· 4 min read

After a marathon session, parliament voted to pass the government's healthcare reform bill. The legislation will extend coverage to an estimated 4 million people and introduce new caps on out-of-pocket costs.

The opposition has said it will challenge key provisions in court, arguing they exceed the legislature's powers. The prime minister called the vote a historic step and thanked cross-party supporters.
